Data Inventory D4 — rva.gov Drupal Content, Search API, and Structured Data

Provide a technical inventory of rva.gov as a data source for building a service navigation tool. The goal is to understand what can be programmatically accessed and indexed.

Sitemap and crawlability:
- Does rva.gov publish a sitemap.xml? If so, what is the URL?
- How is the site structured for crawling (robots.txt, crawl delay, disallowed paths)?
- What are the typical page formats (HTML, SPA, server-rendered)?
- How many pages are in the site approximately?

Department content:
- How are department landing pages structured? What fields are consistently present (department name, service description, contact, hours, forms)?
- Are there structured templates used for service pages?
- Is there a consistent "service description" content type?

Search API:
- What does the Drupal Search API with Facets expose programmatically?
- Can search results be queried as JSON? What is the response schema?
- What are the 40+ business_unit facet IDs? Where is this list documented?
- What relevance ranking algorithm does the search use?

Structured data and schema.org:
- Does rva.gov use schema.org markup for government services, departments, or contact information?
- Are there JSON-LD blocks in page source?

Content freshness:
- Do rva.gov pages include a "last updated" date in the HTML?
- Is there an RSS feed or other content change notification mechanism?

Output
- Technical crawl and access guide for rva.gov
- Available structured data fields
- Recommended indexing approach for a hackathon team building a search layer
