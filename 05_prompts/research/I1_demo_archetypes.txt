Demo Guidance I1 — Demo Clarity Archetypes for City Hall Tools

Investigate the most effective demo archetypes for civic tech tools at the hackathon pitch level. Focus on what makes a City Hall tool demo compelling vs. confusing to judges in a 3–5 minute presentation.

The core challenge for City Hall demos:
- The problem (miscategorized 311 requests, buried contract data) requires background context that eats demo time
- The solution (routing helper, contract dashboard) is technical and requires live interaction
- The evidence (before/after comparison) is what makes the value visible
- The credibility (correct data, proper disclaimers) is what separates civic tech from vaporware

ARCHETYPE 1 — "Before and After"
Structure: Show the painful current experience → show your solution → quantify the difference
Example: 30 seconds of a resident struggling on rva.gov → 60 seconds of the same resident using your tool → "this is the difference"
When it works: When the current experience is visibly bad and your solution is clearly better
Risks: Current experience must be accurate (don't exaggerate); solution must work live

ARCHETYPE 2 — "Problem Then Proof"
Structure: State the problem with a data point → show your solution directly → show the supporting evidence
Example: "208,000 311 requests last year. We estimate X% were miscategorized. Our tool routes [scenario] correctly."
When it works: When you have a credible data point to anchor the problem claim
Risks: Data must be verifiable; "we estimate" must be honest, not overclaimed

ARCHETYPE 3 — "User Journey"
Structure: Follow a specific realistic user (named persona) through the entire journey with your tool
Example: "Meet Maria. She notices illegal dumping. She doesn't know which department. She uses our tool..."
When it works: When the user story is relatable and the journey is tight
Risks: Persona must be realistic; don't skip the "with our tool" steps

Output
- Assessment of each archetype for the two City Hall problem types
- Recommended archetype for service navigation demos
- Recommended archetype for procurement demos
- Timing guide for each archetype within a 5-minute slot
