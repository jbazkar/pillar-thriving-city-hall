Services Landscape C1 — RVA311 System: Request Types, Volumes, and Channels

Investigate the RVA311 system in detail, using all publicly available information. The system uses AvePoint Citizen Services on Microsoft Dynamics 365 and Azure, launched June 15, 2018. It replaced SeeClickFix. Total 2025 volume reported at 208,216 requests.

What are the ~50+ request types in the current RVA311 system? How are they organized across the 7 department groups? Where is the current category taxonomy publicly documented (rva.gov, AvePoint documentation, press releases)?

What channels does RVA311 support — web portal, mobile app (iOS/Android), phone, in-person? What percentage of requests come through each channel?

What is the integration chain: Citizen → RVA311 (Dynamics 365) → Microsoft BizTalk → Trimble Cityworks (DPW work orders)? What happens to requests for non-DPW departments?

Historical context: The SeeClickFix platform (2014–2018) generated data now in the Socrata dataset (vgg4-hjn8). What do those 2014–2015 categories tell us about the current system? How much has the category structure changed?

AvePoint Citizen Services product: What does AvePoint's official documentation say about the platform's capabilities, API availability, and data access? Is the "no public API" constraint confirmed in official documentation?
